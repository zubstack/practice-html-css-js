SECO DE POLLO

Description:

"Seco de pollo" is one of the most traditional food in Ecuador!
Delicious food!

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 chicken 🍖
  • tomatoes 🍅
  • onions 🧅
  • peppers 🫑
  • potatoes 🥔
  • coriander 🌿
  • bananas 🍌
  • avocados 🥑
  • rice 🍚

Steps:

  1. Cook the chicken, the rice and the potatoes in different cooking pots
  2. Blender tomatoes, onions and peppers
  3. Mix all inside the chicken
  4. Add some coriander, an banana, an avocado and rice
